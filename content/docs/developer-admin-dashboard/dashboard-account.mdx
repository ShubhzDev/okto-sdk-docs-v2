## How to Create an Account

To create a new account for the dashboard:

1. Navigate to the dashboard [login page](https://dashboard.okto.tech/login).

![Dashboard Login Screen](/images/dashboard-img-v2/dashboard-login-screen.png)

2. Enter your preferred email address to login.
3. Enter the OTP you recieved in your inbox to verify your email.

![OTP Verification Screen](/images/dashboard-img-v2/dashboard-otp-screen-v2.png)


5. Once verified, you are logged in to your new dashboard account.

![OTP Verification Screen](/images/dashboard-img-v2/dashboard-home-screen-v2.png)


<Callout title="Note">
Currently, only one default project is associated with one email address.
</Callout>
