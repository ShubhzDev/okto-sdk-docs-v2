The Okto Token **($OKTO)** is the native utility token of the Okto ecosystem. It is designed to play a crucial role in the functionality, security, and governance of the Okto Layer and its various components, including the Decentralized Wallet Network (DWN), Unified Liquidity Layer (ULL), Decentralized Transaction Network (DTN), and the Okto Chain.
