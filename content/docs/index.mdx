## What is Okto SDK?

The **Okto SDK** is your game-changer for Web3 development. This **plug-and-play toolkit** eliminates blockchain complexity, providing a unified solution for onboarding, login, gasless transactions, and complex Web3 intents. Build intuitive, user-friendly experiences and simplify Web3 from start to finish – we’ve got it ALL.
